About!!

As an overly enthusiastic 15 year old problem solver, I stumbled upon coding through school, not the most unconventional introduction but right off the bat, coding peaked my interest. The adrenaline rush in writing a program and the sheer happiness of getting the correct output made me fall in this loop of gratification. Fast forward to this day, I am still stuck in the same state and I do not intend to “terminate” this loop soon enough.

Python was my first programming language and I am quite proficient in the same. In the past 2 years I have worked on multiple projects, participated in online hackathons, competed in coding competitions, solved problems on platforms like hackerrank and codingame. My ability to learn and adapt swiftly is my one true selling point above all. In November 2020, I made a bid on a text parsing project at freelancer on a whim but to my surprise I got it, I knew nothing of scraping and parsing but said yes with an intent to first learn how to do it and then do it, and got my first pay. Which led to me getting another project on Natural Language Processing , A program that returns the most relevant documents from a corpus based on a search query, and I followed the same simple rule again. I bring my technical and problem solving skills with loads of enthusiasm to the table.
